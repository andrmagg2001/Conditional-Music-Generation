# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct
 
## Our Pledge
 
We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all people.
 
This code of conduct outlines our expectations for participant behavior.
 
## Our Standards
 
Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:
 
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
Examples of unacceptable behavior:
 
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Insults or derogatory comments
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Unwelcome sexual attention
## Enforcement
 
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainer at **andrmagg2001@gmail.com**.
 
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
 
The project maintainer is obligated to maintain confidentiality.
